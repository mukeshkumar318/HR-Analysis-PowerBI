# HR HC Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The HR HC Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze employee headcount data across different business units, countries, genders, age groups, and job roles.

This dashboard helps HR teams and management monitor workforce distribution, identify trends, and support strategic decision-making.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Preview

![HR HC Analysis Dashboard](Dashboard.png)

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall employee headcount.
- Monitor employee growth trends over time.
- Compare male and female workforce distribution.
- Analyze headcount by age group.
- Analyze employee distribution across countries.
- Analyze headcount by business unit.
- Understand workforce distribution by job role.
